# Upcoming Rock Music Releases in 2025

### 1. "Magma" by Deafheaven
   - **Release Date**: January 2025
   - **Genre**: Blackgaze
   - **Notes**: Known for their atmospheric soundscapes, Deafheaven promises an exploration of deep emotions wrapped in heavy guitar layers.

### 2. "Electric Eye" by Slash featuring Myles Kennedy
   - **Release Date**: February 2025
   - **Genre**: Hard Rock
   - **Notes**: This album features hard-hitting riffs paired with soulful vocals, a classic blend that fans expect from Slash.

### 3. "Chasing Shadows" by The Cranberries
   - **Release Date**: March 2025
   - **Genre**: Alternative Rock
   - **Notes**: The band's new sound builds on their legacy, combining nostalgia with contemporary vibes that resonate with past audiences.

### 4. "Songs from the 60s" by Hayley Williams
   - **Release Date**: April 2025
   - **Genre**: Rock
   - **Notes**: A tribute to the classics, Hayley's unique spin on the iconic hits is eagerly anticipated by her fan base.

   - **Release Date**: December 2025
   - **Genre**: Progressive Rock
   - **Notes**: This collection includes remastered tracks from their legendary discography, appealing to both old fans and new listeners.

### Conclusion
Expect a year filled with diverse rock sounds as artists explore fresh creative directions while honoring their roots.
